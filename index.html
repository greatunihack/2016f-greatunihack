About Us

We are GreatUniHack, an annual 24-hour student-oriented hackathon organised by the University of Manchester and Manchester Metropolitan University's hackathon societies.

Established in 2014, we have brought together students from 88 universities across Europe to work and develop innovative ideas in a competitive environmnent. This year's edition aims to introduce a new generation of developers with great potential for improving the technology industry and community.
